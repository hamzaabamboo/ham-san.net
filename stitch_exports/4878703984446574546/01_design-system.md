## Design Direction

The design system is named `The Builder's Atelier` and describes a dark, industrial-premium visual language with:

- strict `0px` radius
- exposed structural borders
- grayscale surfaces with amber as the action/signal color
- editorial serif headings paired with robust sans UI text
- dense, architectural layouts
- hard-edged elevation rather than soft ambient blur
