# Design Language

The Astro implementation must match the exported Stitch design language for project `4878703984446574546`.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Pages use a dark industrial-premium visual language with hard edges, exposed borders, and no soft ambient styling
- The accent color behaves like a signal color anchored in radiant amber and applied selectively
- Surfaces remain grayscale-first with strong contrast against the background
- Headings read as editorial serif while interface text reads as robust sans
- The brand mark reads as a structural letter `H` with drafting-tool precision
- The experience avoids invented naming or decorative concepts not present in the Stitch comps
