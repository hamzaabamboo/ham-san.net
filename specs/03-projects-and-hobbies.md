# Projects And Hobbies Experience

The projects and hobbies routes must reproduce the visual rhythm and content framing of the exported Stitch screens.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The projects page separates work using the verified data model available in CMS; do not rename active/archive into commercial/personal without a real discriminator
- Project cards feel archival and precise rather than playful or app-store-like
- Project display copy may clean known upstream CMS typos/incomplete descriptions at the presentation layer, but must not invent project facts
- The hobbies page uses a composed grid with varied module sizes and clear editorial hierarchy
- Hobbies cards must be full-width grid items at every breakpoint; missing media must render deliberate fallback art
- Supporting labels, metadata, and calls to action remain terse and aligned with the Stitch tone
- The polished hobbies route quality is the target for the standard hobbies experience
