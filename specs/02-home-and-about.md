# Home And About Experience

The localized home and about routes must reflect the structure, density, and tone of the exported Stitch homepage and dossier screens.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The homepage presents a dense architectural composition rather than a generic marketing hero
- The homepage includes a strong primary identity block, a visual system block, and supporting modules arranged with clear structural hierarchy
- The about page reads like a dossier with institutional framing, disciplined metadata, and compact narrative sections
- The pages keep the same tonal direction in English, Japanese, and Thai without awkward literal phrasing
- Route-level chrome, spacing, and typography stay visually aligned with the Stitch comps
