# Note Detail And Contact Experience

The note detail and contact routes must match the exported Stitch compositions and maintain the same editorial-industrial tone.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Note detail pages present article content inside a structured archival frame with visible metadata and strong navigation affordances
- Real note detail content must not expand the mobile document viewport; wide tables/images scroll inside the article area
- Stale or unavailable note slugs render a designed unavailable state at the requested route instead of dumping to generic 404
- Contact pages present identity, channels, and contact actions as designed system modules rather than a generic form page
- Namecard-style presentation appears as part of the contact experience
- Contact form behavior must match implementation reality: required fields and `mailto:` transport unless a real backend exists
- Contact/security copy must not claim encryption, private server routing, or live delivery behavior that is not implemented
- Dense visual framing is preserved on desktop and remains coherent on mobile
